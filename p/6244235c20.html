İstanbul Guide: Everything About Antique Shops

If someone told me a year ago that İstanbul would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

I documented my entire antique shops experience in İstanbul through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.

The infrastructure around antique shops in İstanbul is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely. this is absolutely worth your time.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, İstanbul offers a more intimate and genuine antique shops experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

İstanbul reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.
