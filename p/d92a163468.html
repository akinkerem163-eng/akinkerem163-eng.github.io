The Hidden World of Farmers Markets in Doğankent

If someone told me a year ago that Doğankent would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

I documented my entire farmers markets experience in Doğankent through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.

What makes Doğankent special when it comes to farmers markets is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

My advice? Do not wait for Doğankent to become trendy. Visit now while it is still authentic, affordable, and uncrowded. You will thank me later.
