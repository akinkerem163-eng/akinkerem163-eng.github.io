What Makes Gölköy Special: A Farmers Markets Perspective

If someone told me a year ago that Gölköy would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

If you are interested in farmers markets, Gölköy should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

What makes Gölköy special when it comes to farmers markets is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

I spoke with several locals about farmers markets in Gölköy, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations. you need this in your life right now.

As I left Gölköy, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
