Discovering İstanbul: My Artisan Workshops Adventure

If someone told me a year ago that İstanbul would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

I spoke with several locals about artisan workshops in İstanbul, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations. the internet at its finest right here.

The artisan workshops of İstanbul reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here.

The artisan workshops scene in İstanbul is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

My advice? Do not wait for İstanbul to become trendy. Visit now while it is still authentic, affordable, and uncrowded. You will thank me later.
