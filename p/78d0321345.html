Exploring Bazaars in Kilis: A Personal Account

There are places in Turkey that time seems to have forgotten, and Kilis is one of them. Walking through its streets felt like stepping into a living museum.

I spoke with several locals about bazaars in Kilis, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Kilis offers a more intimate and genuine bazaars experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity. this is the kind of stuff that restores my faith in the internet.

If you are interested in bazaars, Kilis should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

As I left Kilis, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
